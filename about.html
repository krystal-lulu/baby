Konig Kids(Shenzhen) Limited was founded as the headquarter of our group in 2015 based in Shenzhen,China specializes in supplying baby products and toys that enhance and accompany erery baby in the first steps of life.Here at Konig,we have the best in sales representation and products development to insure we make a difference and we are proud of offering to customers,new,high quality,safe and innovative products. As each new standard become effective over the time in each area across the world,we will continue to meet or exceed such standards at each reuqired date.

The mission of Konig is to bring joy into the lives of its customers and focused on fully transforming the customer experience; developing high-performing and highly focused teams; collaborating with business partners to drive differentiation, innovation and value, and positioning the business for growth. At Konig, we love kids! Since our group founding more than 15 years ago, kids have been central to who we are and what we do. We approach our business operations with responsibility and integrity, understanding the trust customer’s place in us to do the right thing and act as a reliable partner as they navigate the various stages of parenthood.
