Exercise should pay attention to heart rate, contractions Expectant mothers are thinking about: how many sports do you want?Takahane feel, judge whether exercise for oneself, whether from fatigue, heart rate and best ways to assess uterine contractions.Normally when the heart rate increased to more than 100 per minute, then physical strength can't follow you, don't increase the amount of exercise. At the time of movement to observe uterine contractions.Just pregnant, uterine contractions, a sense of the lower abdomen of the belly, similar to menstrual cramps;In the middle-late, contractions are found to be more obvious, belly will feel very hard, there will be a rush of blood to the head. At the time of movement, if less than 30 minutes to appear once more regular and frequent uterine contractions, so had better take a rest at this moment.Many mothers-to-be contractions may slowly fade, but if do not fade for long contractions had better be to go immediately to the hospital inspection, the possibility of sustained contractions have certain can lead to premature birth, abortion or rupture.
